Sayyed-us-Sadaat, Qasim-ul-Khairaat, Hazrat Peer Sayyed

Feroz Shah Qasimi Qadiri Naqshbandi Tirmizi Buneri (D.B.A.)

Sayyed-us-Sadaat (Guru Lord & authority), Qasimul Khairat (Distributor of charity) Hazrat Pir Sayyed Feroz Shah Qasimi Qadiri Naqshbandi Tirmizi Buneri (Damat Barakatuhumul Aalia) is fulfilling the seekers of mystics according to the Qadria & Naqshbandia schools of thought in Karachi (Natha Khan Goth).

He is Husaini Sayyed and descendent of Rev. Syed Ali Ghawwas Tirmizi alias Pir Baba Buneri (PBUH). Rev. Pir Baba had the blessings of 7 chains and he was revivalist of 3 chains.

He is blessed with the devotion of his guide and besides many of his favors, possesses the benevolence of 7 links of the chain if we see him he is the true and complete interpretation of ‘Iza Ru`oo Zukirallah’. He is an embodiment of Re. Yusuf’s beauty and model of the Holy Prophet’s (P.B.U.H.) tradition. He is an extremely serious and resolute personality.

Rev. beloved spiritual guide was born in village Shalbandi, Tehsil Dagar, Distt. Buner (Swat), His geneological saintly line reached Rev. Maula Ali, Lion of God (R.A.A).

His Illustrious Majesty Hazrat Pir Syed Feroz Shah Qasmi ( Damat Barakata Humul Allaiah) the most clairvoyant, sacred, religious and spiritual of this age requires no introduction as a successor of devout saints who spent every moment of their lives being lost in the contemplation of Allah and His beloved Prophet Hazrat Muhammed (Sallalah-u-alaih-e-wa-alehi-wasalam)The seekers of Divinity , mysticism and the lovers of Holy Prophet (PBUH) (Sallalah-u-alaih-e-wa-alehi-wasalam) are being imbued with spirituality and bounties (faiz) at Dargah-e-Aaliah Qasmia Ferozia Ahl-e-Sunnat Pakistan (Trust) .

The Organization, Jamat-e-Qasmia Ferozia Ahl-e-Sunnat Pakistan (Trust) is comprised of such sensible and meditative followers who, under the Guidance of Hazrat Pir Syed Feroz Shah Qasmi (Damat Barakata Humul Allaiah) based on complete codes of Shariah (Islamic Law) and Tareeqat (way to recognize The Almighty through implementing Shariah), have adhered themselves with eternal path of Ishq-e-Muhammadi (SAWW), The True love of The Holy Prophet (PBUH) . This Organization, based on the principles of Shariat, Tareeqat, Marfat and Haqeeqat, has been busy in fulfilling the thirst of those people who are in pursuit of spirituality.
